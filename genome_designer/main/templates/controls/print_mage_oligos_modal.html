This form allows you to design MAGE[1] oligos corresponding to the variant set. Please specify the replicaction origin and terminus so that the oligo sense is optimized, as well as the experiment direction - whether you are introducing or reverting changes.

[1] Wang, Harris H., et al. "Programming cells by multiplex genome engineering and accelerated evolution." Nature 460.7257 (2009): 894-898.

Disclaimers

  • All oligos are printed on the reference genome context. That means that other new alleles in your strain may be reverted.
  • Variants within 90 bp of each other may toggle each other back and forth. For now, we just flag such variants in the output.
